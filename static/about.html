About Food Sensei

Empowering You to Make Healthier Food Choices with AI

Food Sensei is an AI-driven platform designed to help users make healthier food choices by analyzing food items and providing valuable nutritional information. Our app uses cutting-edge technology to categorize food, provide health labels, and offer personalized suggestions based on user preferences and local food availability.
